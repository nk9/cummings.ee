Post Impressions I

windows go orange in the slowly.
town, night
featherly swifts
the
Dark on us
all;
stories told returned

gather

the

Again:who
danc ing
goes utter ly

churning
witty,twitters

upon Our

(ta-te-ta
in a parenthesis!said the moon
