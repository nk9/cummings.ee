Post Impressions III

into the strenuous briefness
Life:
handorgans and April
darkness, friends

i charge laughing.
Into the hair-thing tints
of yellow dawn,
into the women-coloured twilight

i smilingly
glide.
into the big vermillion departure
swim, sayingly;

(Do you think?) the
i do, world
is probably made
of roses & hello:

(of solongs and, ashes)
