e.e. cummings free poetry archive

Tulips and Chimneys

This 1923 release was Cummings’s first book of verse. It entered the public domain in 2019.

The first edition, published by Thomas Seltzer, is reproduced here. It omitted much of the author’s early works, which would surface in subsequent books and later editions.
